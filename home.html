How It Works...

To use our website, simply follow these steps:

  1. Browse the available costume services.
  2. Select the costume you like.
  3. Add the costume to your cart.
  4. Select only from available size and color from the product card.
  5. Fill in the necessary details and complete the order process.
  6. Receive your costume and enjoy!

What is the Process?

Browse

Explore a wide range of items available in our catalog. Use the search and filter options to find exactly what you’re looking for.

Cart

Add your desired items to the cart. Review your selections and proceed to checkout.

Payment

Choose your payment method from options such as Bkash, Nagad, Rocket, or Cash on Delivery.

Delivery

Once the payment is confirmed, your items will be delivered to the provided address in 24 hours.

Return

If needed, you can return the items according to our return policy.
