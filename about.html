Privacy Policy

Welcome to GorgeousGrab, your one-stop destination for the best costume rental services. We offer a wide range of costumes to suit every occasion. Whether you're attending a themed party or need a costume for a performance, we've got you covered. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is what sets us apart. Our team is always ready to help you find the perfect outfit for your needs. Thank you for choosing GorgeousGrab! Below are the terms of our privacy policy:

  • We collect personal data such as name, email, and contact information for the purpose of processing orders.
  • All payment details are encrypted and stored securely through trusted third-party providers.
  • Your personal information is never shared with unauthorized parties.
  • Delivery times and rental fees will be communicated upon confirmation of orders.
  • Customers are responsible for returning costumes in good condition, and damage fees may apply if necessary.
  • Rental durations vary based on the selected package and must be adhered to as outlined in the rental agreement.

Our rental policy ensures that you receive your costume on time and in perfect condition. We charge a small fee for delivery, and any damages must be reported immediately. The rental duration is typically three days, with additional charges applied for late returns. Our privacy policy protects your personal information, ensuring it is only used for order processing and communication. We do not share your data with third parties. Thank you for trusting us with your rental needs.
